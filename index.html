WBG FCV Analytics Unit

FCV Sensitivity Project Screener

A tool for screening WBG projects for recommendations to strengthen FCV sensitivity and responsiveness

What this tool does

This tool uses an AI language model to screen draft WBG project documents for FCV dynamics. It generates structured recommendations on how to strengthen FCV sensitivity and responsiveness, drawing on the uploaded documents, the WBG FCV Sensitivity & Responsiveness Guide, and the AI's broader knowledge of the country and sector context.

The tool is designed to support — not replace — expert FCV analysis. Stage 4 includes an Explore FCV-Sensitive Solutions panel where you can drill into specific, actionable project design options for each strategic priority.

How to use it — four stages
Stage 1 · Identifying FCV Risks

Upload your PCN or PAD. The AI extracts all FCV-relevant content and flags country context from its training knowledge.

Stage 2 · FCV-Sensitivity Screening

The AI assesses the project across six FCV dimensions, rating risks both to and from the project.

Stage 3 · Identifying Project Gaps

The AI identifies gaps in FCV sensitivity and design, proposes mitigations, and runs a do-no-harm checklist.

Stage 4 · Recommendations Note + Explorer

A collegial peer-review memo with strategic priorities, plus an on-demand Explore FCV-Sensitive Solutions panel for concrete, specific design options per priority.

Important — use with care
  • Outputs are generated by an AI and may contain errors, omissions, or misjudgements.
  • The AI draws on general knowledge of the country context, which may be outdated or incomplete.
  • The Recommendations Note should be reviewed, adapted, and validated by an FCV specialist before any operational use.
  • Do not upload classified, confidential, or otherwise restricted documents.
